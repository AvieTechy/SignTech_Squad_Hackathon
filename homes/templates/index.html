FAQ

How accurate is Align?

Align is based on a deep neural network that can learn from a large dataset of sign language videos. However, sign language is not a fixed system, but a dynamic and expressive one that can vary depending on the context, the region, and the individual. Therefore, our product may not be able to capture all the nuances and variations of sign language. We are constantly working to improve our accuracy and expand our coverage of different sign languages. üöÄ

How do I use Align with my webcam?

You will see yourself on the screen, along with the translation in text below. Make sure you have enough light and space, and that your hands are visible and clear on the screen.
